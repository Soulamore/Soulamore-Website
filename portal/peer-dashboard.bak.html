Peer Support Guidelines

Why These Guidelines Matter

Peer support sessions are the heart of Soulamore. They provide a safe space for connection. To ensure effectiveness and integrity, it's essential to adhere to these core principles.

Confidentiality
Respect the confidentiality of all participants. What is shared here stays here, unless there is an immediate risk of harm.
Active Listening
Give your full attention without interruption. Validate feelings and refrain from offering unsolicited advice. Let them express fully.
Empathy & Compassion
Acknowledge emotions without trying to "fix" them. Validation creates connection. Everyone's journey is unique.
Boundaries
Avoid prying into details beyond what they share. If someone is uncomfortable, respect their boundary and shift the conversation.
Non-Directive Approach
Encourage self-reflection using open-ended questions. Help them explore their own thoughts rather than telling them what to do.
Safety & Crisis
Prioritize safety. If there is risk of self-harm, connect them with professional resources immediately. Escalate concerns.
