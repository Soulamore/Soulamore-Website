Terms of Use

Welcome to Soulamore. By accessing or using this website, you agree to the terms outlined below.

What Soulamore is

Soulamore is a mental wellness and peer-support platform built around conversation, reflection, and shared human experiences.

Soulamore is NOT:

  • A medical service
  • A therapy clinic
  • A crisis or emergency response platform

Using the platform responsibly

By using Soulamore, you agree to:

  • Engage respectfully with others
  • Share content responsibly and honestly
  • Avoid abusive, harmful, misleading, or threatening content
  • Respect boundaries set for Peers and Psychologists

Moderation & Access

Soulamore reserves the right to review, edit, or remove content that violates guidelines and restrict access in cases of misuse.

For questions regarding these terms: contact.soulamore@gmail.com

Disclaimer

Soulamore is a peer-support and conversation platform. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care, medical advice, or emergency services.

Important Clarification

  • Peers are not therapists
  • Psychologists on the platform do not provide emergency care
  • Content is shared for reflection and support only

Emergency Situations

If you are experiencing suicidal thoughts, risk of self-harm, or immediate danger, please contact:

  • Your nearest hospital
  • A licensed mental health professional
  • A local emergency helpline

Support
