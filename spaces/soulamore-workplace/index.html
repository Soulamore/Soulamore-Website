Workplace Guidelines

Our Core Values

At Soulamore, we believe that a healthy workplace starts with clear, compassionate communication and respect for boundaries.

1. Anonymity First

What is shared with a Peer stays with a Peer. We do not track individual user conversations or report specific grievances to HR unless there is an immediate threat.

2. Respectful Engagement

All interactions on the Soulamore platform must be respectful. Harassment, hate speech, or abuse will result in immediate suspension.

3. Not for Crisis

Soulamore is a peer support and wellness platform, not an emergency service. In danger? Contact emergency services.
