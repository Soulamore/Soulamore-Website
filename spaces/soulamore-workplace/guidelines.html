Workplace Guidelines

Our Core Values

At Soulamore, we believe that a healthy workplace starts with clear, compassionate communication and respect for boundaries. These guidelines are designed to create a safe space for all employees.

1. Anonymity First

What is shared with a Peer stays with a Peer. We do not track individual user conversations or report specific grievances to HR unless there is an immediate threat to life or safety.

2. Respectful Engagement

All interactions on the Soulamore platform must be respectful. Harassment, hate speech, or abuse of Peers will result in immediate suspension of access.

3. Not for Crisis

Soulamore is a peer support and wellness platform, not an emergency service. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, please contact emergency services or use the "Get Help Now" button for crisis helplines.

Usage Policy

Eligibility

Access is provided to all full-time employees and their immediate family members (if covered under the Premium plan).

Session Limits

  • Chats: Unlimited 24/7 access to Peer Listeners.
  • Consultations: Subject to plan limits (e.g., Lite vs Premium). Additional sessions can be purchased at a corporate discount.

Data Privacy

We adhere to strict data privacy standards. Aggregated, non-identifiable mood data may be shared with leadership to help improve overall company culture, but your individual voice remains private.
