Emergency Toolkit

3 things you can do in under 2 minutes to reset your brain.

01
The 90-Sec Rule

Emotions are chemicals. They flush out in 90 seconds if you don't fight them.

  • Stop moving.
  • Breathe deep (4 counts in, 4 out).
  • Wait for the "wave" to pass.
02
5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

For panic attacks. Force your brain to switch from "feeling" to "sensing".

  • 5 things you see.
  • 4 things you can touch.
  • 3 things you hear.
03
Brain Dump

Can't sleep? Your brain is afraid it will forget your worries.

  • Write worries on paper.
  • Tell brain: "It's safe on paper."
  • Leave paper in other room.
