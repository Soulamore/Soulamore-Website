Clinical Accuracy

Every assessment on Soulamore is structured using globally recognized psychological benchmarks. We prioritize accuracy and clinical integrity in everything we build.

GAD-7 Scale

General Anxiety Disorder (GAD-7)

The GAD-7 is a seven-item instrument used as a screening tool and severity measure for generalized anxiety disorder. It is used extensively in clinical settings and research worldwide.

Source: Spitzer RL, et al. Arch Intern Med. 2006.
PHQ-9 Scale

Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9)

The PHQ-9 is a multipurpose instrument for screening, diagnosing, monitoring, and measuring the severity of depression. It incorporates DSM-IV depression diagnostic criteria.

Source: Kroenke K, et al. J Gen Intern Med. 2001.
MBI Scale

Maslach Burnout Inventory (MBI)

The MBI is the first scientifically developed measure of burnout and is used in research and practice worldwide to assess three dimensions of burnout: Emotional Exhaustion, Depersonalization, and Personal Accomplishment.

Source: Maslach C, et al. 1981.
