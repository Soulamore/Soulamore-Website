Data Collection & Usage

Our privacy policy delineates the detailed practices we employ regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your data.

What We Collect

  • We collect information provided by you during registration, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, and relevant background information necessary for therapy sessions.
  • We do not collect sensitive data such as financial information or medical records, ensuring a focus solely on the therapeutic process.

How We Use It

  • The information collected is utilized to facilitate therapy sessions, personalize your user experience, and enhance the quality of our services.

Data Security

We employ state-of-the-art security protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Despite our rigorous efforts, it is important to acknowledge that no online platform can guarantee absolute security, and users should exercise caution when sharing personal information online.

Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Soulamore places paramount importance on the confidentiality of the therapeutic process. Every therapist on our platform is ethically and legally bound by a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), ensuring the sanctity of the therapeutic relationship.

Confidentiality Assurance

  • Therapists are strictly prohibited from discussing or disclosing any information shared during therapy sessions to third parties, including other therapists, staff, or external entities.
  • Any breach of this confidentiality clause is treated with the utmost seriousness, and therapists are held accountable for maintaining the highest ethical standards.

Platform Usage

  • Soulamore provides a platform designed to connect individuals seeking therapeutic support with licensed professionals and peer supporters.
  • Users acknowledge that Soulamore acts solely as a facilitator, providing a secure and supportive space for therapeutic interactions and is not intended to substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Service Fees

Soulamore facilitates connections between individuals and professionals. In exchange for the platform's services, Soulamore deducts 50% of the professional fee for each session booked. This fee covers technology infrastructure, customer support, and marketing efforts.

Liability & Integrity

User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for the confidentiality of their account information, including passwords. Users agree to provide accurate information when registering.

Limitation of Liability

Soulamore shall not be held liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages arising from the use or inability to use our services. Use of Soulamore is at your sole risk and discretion.

Platform Integrity

Individual therapists, counselors, and peer supporters are independent professionals and are neither owned nor employed by Soulamore. Soulamore reserves the right to terminate any user's account in the event of violations of community guidelines.
