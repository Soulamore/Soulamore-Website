IMPORTANT PRIVACY PROMISE

We prioritize your anonymity. We do NOT save your Name, Email, or Phone Number unless you explicitly provide them for login or contact. Your personal details are never linked to anonymous submissions. Here is how specific tools handle your data:

  • The Vent Box: Anonymous text is stored to help us identify community stress patterns, but never linked to your ID.
  • Release a Thought: Quick thoughts are anonymized and counted globally.
  • The Worry Shredder: Worries released here are anonymized and permanently shredded from your view.
  • The Void / Echo: Submissions are anonymous and used to understand collective feelings.
  • Confession Box: Personal details are optional. We only save them if you chose to.
  • SoulBot AI: Chat memory is retained to enhance conversation but is never used to identify you personally.
  • Newsletters: Emails provided are used only for communication.

Data Collection & Usage

We only collect what is strictly necessary to provide you with a safe space:

  • Account Info: When you sign up, we verify your email or phone number to prevent spam and ensure safety.
  • Profile Data: Any nickname, bio, role (User/Peer/Pro) or preferences you choose to share.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous metrics (e.g., "Vent Box used 50 times") to improve the platform.

We do NOT sell your personal data to advertisers.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to control your digital footprint on Soulamore:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: You can update your profile details at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account entirely from the Settings page. This action is permanent.
  • Anonymity: You have the right to use a pseudonym.

Cookies

We keep it calm and minimal.

  • Essential Cookies: To keep you logged in securely (Firebase Auth).
  • Functional Cookies: To remember your Light/Dark mode preference.
  • No Tracking: We do not use intrusive 3rd-party tracking cookies for ads.

Security & Professional Ethics

We employ state-of-the-art security to protect your data.

Therapist NDA

Every Psychologist on our platform is bound by a strict Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA).

  • Therapists cannot discuss your sessions with third parties.
  • Any breach of this confidentiality is treated with legal seriousness.
