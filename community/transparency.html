"Is Soulamore Legit?"

We've seen the threads. We hear the questions. Here is the direct answer to everything the Reddit community asks about us.

Is Soulamore actually anonymous?

Yes. Unlike traditional platforms that require your phone number or real name to start, Soulamore is built on a "Privacy First" architecture. We don't sell your data to advertisers because our mission is emotional safety, not ad-targeting profiles. We use ephemeral sessions and localized storage to ensure your "venting" stays yours.

Are the "Peers" real people or AI?

Real people. While we have SoulBot for immediate clinical guidance and structured assessments, our peer spaces are for human-to-human connection. We believe that technology should facilitate human empathy, not replace it. Every peer mentor undergoes a verification process to ensure a safe environment.

How do you handle "Scary AI" fears?

We've received feedback that some find current AI "creepy." We hear you. Our AI (SoulBot) is strictly a Clinical Companion. It doesn't pretend to have a soul or a personal life. It is a tool designed to map your emotional state using clinical benchmarks like GAD-7, providing data-driven insights while leaving the "soul" work to you and the community.

Why is Soulamore free (or affordable)?

Mental health shouldn't be a luxury for the top 1%. Our primary goal is accessibility for Indian students and professionals. We maintain our operations through B2B partnerships and premium "Deep-Dive" reports, keeping the core peer support and basic assessments accessible to everyone who needs them.
