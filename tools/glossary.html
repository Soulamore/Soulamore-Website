Wellness Glossary

Anxiety

Anticipatory Stress

The feeling of dread or nervous energy directed toward a future event. Clinically distinct from Generalized Anxiety, as it is strictly tied to 'impending' timelines.

Burnout

Emotional Depersonalization

A core symptom of burnout where an individual feels detached from their work or the people they serve, viewing them through a lens of cynicism or apathy.

Cognitive

Catastrophizing

A cognitive distortion where an individual identifies a potential problem and immediately jumps to the worst possible outcome, regardless of probability.

Relationships

Secure Attachment

An emotional bond characterized by trust, an ability to share feelings openly, and a healthy balance of independence and intimacy within a relationship.

Therapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

A structured, goal-oriented psychotherapy that focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns to improve emotional regulation.

India Specific

Cultural Stoicism

The cultural tendency to suppress emotional distress in order to maintain family harmony or social standing, often leading to internalized stress.
