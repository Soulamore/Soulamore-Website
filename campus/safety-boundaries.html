Safety is not optional.
It is foundational.

While we encourage openness, we are equally clear about boundaries. Vulnerable spaces need structure to remain safe.

What Soulamore Campus IS

  • A peer-supported mental wellness ecosystem
  • A space for expression, reflection, and guided support
  • A bridge between silence and professional care

What Soulamore Campus is NOT

  • A crisis hotline
  • A replacement for medical or psychiatric treatment
  • An unmoderated emotional outlet

When a situation requires professional care, we escalate responsibly.

Safeguarding & Escalation Protocol

1. Detection
Peer or system notices concern via confession, journal, or conversation.
2. Counselor Review
Distinguishes distress from immediate risk.
3. Psychologist Intervention
For professional assessment and guidance.
4. Guardian Informed
Parents/Guardians informed if risk is significant or ongoing.
5. External Support Activated
Tele-MANAS (14416), child protection services, or emergency care when required.

This ensures no student is left alone, and no peer is burdened beyond their role.
