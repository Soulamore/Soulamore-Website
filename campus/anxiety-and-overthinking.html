An anxious mind is not a broken mind. It is a mind that learned to stay alert to survive.

Anxiety says: "I don't feel safe yet."

First: Calm the Body

Your brain doesn't respond to "Just relax." It responds to breath. Try to match the circle.

Breathe

Longer exhale = Calmer nervous system.

The "Later Box" Trick

Trying to stop thoughts makes them louder. Instead, "park" them for later.
