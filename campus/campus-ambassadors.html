Where You Already Are.

This isn’t about titles or certificates. It’s about noticing, listening, and not letting people feel alone.

Is this you?

(Tap what feels true. No right answers.)

You’re already doing the work.

This program just helps you do it with support.

What Ambassadors Actually Do

No grand gestures. No fixing people. Just human things.

Start Conversations
The kind that make it easier for others to open up.
Share Resources
So people don’t have to search alone in the dark.
Create Safe Moments
Study rooms. Walks. Check-ins. Small pauses.
Refer, Don't Fix
Know when to guide them to help. You don't carry it alone.
You are not expected to have answers.
You are expected to notice.

Your First Micro Action

If you were to start tomorrow, what would you do?

Share a Soulamore post
Start a small discussion
Put up a poster
Just listen better
There’s no “correct” choice. There’s only starting.

What You'll Receive

Training on listening without taking on burdens
Clear boundaries so you don’t burn out
A support system behind you
Space to say "this is too much" when it is

You don’t need to decide today. You just need to be open.
