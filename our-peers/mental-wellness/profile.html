A little about me

I’m here for conversations that feel tangled, slow, or unfinished. I know what it’s like when thoughts don’t stop looping, and when explaining yourself feels harder than just staying quiet.

You don’t need to arrive with clarity. We can find it together, or simply sit with what is. I’ve navigated my own path through academic pressure and burnout, and I understand the weight of expectations.

This space may feel right if...
  • You're overthinking small things that don’t feel small.
  • You feel emotionally tired but aren't sure why.
  • You're carrying things you haven’t said out loud yet.
  • You want a calm listener, not a problem-solver.
  • You aren't ready for therapy, but don't want to be alone.
How I show up
Slow-paced Gentle questions Comfortable with silence Reflective Patient
What our conversation looks like
  • We start wherever you are (no agenda).
  • You can talk, type, or take breaks.
  • I won’t interrupt or rush you.
  • There’s no “right” outcome expected.

A gentle note on boundaries

Peers at Soulamore don’t diagnose or treat mental health conditions. If you’re feeling unsafe or in crisis, I’ll help you find immediate professional support. This space is about being heard, not being fixed.


You can begin with a single sentence. Or none at all.
