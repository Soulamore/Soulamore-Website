Our Peers

At Soulamore, peers are not here to fix you. They are here to sit with you, listen without judgement, and help you untangle what feels heavy.

How to choose: It’s not about finding the "best" person. It’s about finding someone who feels close enough to your experience. Take your time. There’s no rush.

Gentle Boundary Note

Peers at Soulamore are trained listeners, not clinical therapists. They don’t diagnose or treat mental health conditions.

If you’re feeling unsafe, in crisis, or at risk of harming yourself, we’ll gently guide you to immediate professional help. You deserve the right kind of support.
