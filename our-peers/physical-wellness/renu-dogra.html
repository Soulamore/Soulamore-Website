Meet Renu Dogra

Hello, I'm Renu. My goal is to create a supportive and transformative environment for practitioners of all levels. I specialize in Hatha, Vinyasa, and Ashtanga yoga to ensure a well-rounded practice.

My classes focus on promoting strength, flexibility, and mindfulness, helping you connect deeply with your inner self. In addition to physical practice, I incorporate elements of meditation and breathwork.
